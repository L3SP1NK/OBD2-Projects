The AppName Dash Echo Server allows you to connect TunerStudio to your dashboard for tuning and data logging of your ECU.
No need to disconnect your dash from your ECU when you want to tune. You can also connect additional AppName for multiple screens. In fact, you can connect multiple dashboards and multiple laptops running TunerStudio all at the same time! Any tuning changes made by any laptop are propagated to all other connected devices. All is kept in sync by the Dash Echo Server.

All that is needed is for all devices to be on the same WiFi network. That network can be served by the dashboard itself.
