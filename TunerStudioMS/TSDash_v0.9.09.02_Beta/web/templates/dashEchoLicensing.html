Dash Echo Licensing is managed on a per connection/Seat basis. Each license allow 1 additional simultaneous client. To connect TunerStudio for Tuning and data logging requires 1 license. If you want to tune from 2 TunerStudio's at the same time, that requires licensing for at least 2 Seats. Adding a 2nd AppName that connects through the primary AppName, requires 1 Seat.
